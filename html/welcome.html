Bookmark tree chrome extension - gives you ability to make you bookmarks live. With the help of this extension you can easily annotate any text content, add comments or add link to other source. After that bookmark becomes more than just a bookmark - it contains the snapshot of your thoughts.
After saving bookmarks are available from you any devica at any time
With Bookmark tree chrome extension you can share you bookmarks. Grant write or just view to you friends. You can easily collaborate on bookmark. After user performes modification on shared bookmark - you will get notification. Thus this is easy to tracks changes.
