to Bookmark Tree chrome extension
Bookmark tree chrome extension - gives you ability to make you bookmarks live. With the help of this extension you can easily annotate any text content, add comments or add link to other source. After that bookmark becomes more than just a bookmark - it contains the snapshot of your thoughts.
After saving bookmarks are available from you any device at any time
With Bookmark tree chrome extension you can share you bookmarks. Grant write or just view rights to you friends. You can easily collaborate on bookmark. After user performes modification on shared bookmark - you will get notification. Thus, this is easy to tracks changes.

Bookmark tree chrome extension - has simple yet powerful tools.

With tool "pen" - you can mark any text you want. You also can change its color on the Preference tab.

With tool "comment" - you can leave any comment you want.

Tool "link" allows you to create a link to another web page.

With the help of these tools you can make information of the bookmark as useful as possible. From the "home" tab you also can navigate to the web-app to see you bookmarks.

Bookmark tree allows you easily share you annotated bookmarks with the world. You can either share bookmark with read only rights or with owner rights. If you shared your bookmark with owner rights - you friend will be able to modify it. After modifications all owners of the bookmark will get notifications.

You can easily tune extension as you wish. On the settings tab you can change color of the marker. Also you can disable comments, links or markers. You also can set custom synchronization period. Synchronization period is the period your extension synchronizes with cloud. So, after making bookmark on the one machine, it will be available after given synchronization period on your other machine automatically.
